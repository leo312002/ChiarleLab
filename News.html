03/17
2013
A novel technique on genome-wide mapping of DNA double-strand breaks was recently published on Nature Methods

This work was finished by collaboration of several groups respectively from Europe, MIT, UTMB and our lab. The published paper brightly presented a novel genome-wide approach to map DNA double-strand breaks (DSBs) at nucleotide resolution by a method termed BLESS (direct in situ Breaks Labeling, Enrichment on Streptavidin and next-generation Sequencing).
